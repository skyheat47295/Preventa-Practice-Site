You Can Advance Early Cancer Detection to Save Millions of Lives!

Preventa America's new Better Cancer Outcomes program is recruiting active cancer patients to donate blood samples for testing in a groundbreaking study to find a blood marker that can identify potential cancer cells a decade before they become a tumor.

The importance of discovering such a blood marker cannot be overstated. When a simple blood test can identify cancer cells up to 10 years before it turns into the disease, people will have the time to make the lifestyle and healthstyle changes that will prevent those cancer cells from developing. People will have the time to live their best lives.


Helping prevent cancer and reduce its heavy toll on people and families has been the driving force of my work and the work of Dr. Gabor Somlyai president of the Preventa and the unquestioned leader in cancer treatments with deuterium depleted water. We hope you will join us in supporting this important research. Together we can stop cancer before it starts.
